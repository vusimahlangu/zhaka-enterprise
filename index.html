Meet Our Founder

Sandile Xhaka

Sandile Xhaka is the visionary founder of Zhaka Enterprise, an innovative and community-driven business based in Embalenhle, South Africa. With a deep passion for business and a commitment to empowering local communities, Sandile established Zhaka Enterprise on 29 October 2025 to address the growing demand for quality products while creating opportunities for economic growth.

Sandile's entrepreneurial spirit and dedication to excellence have been the driving forces behind the success of Zhaka Enterprise. With hands-on experience and a strong understanding of customer needs in the market, Sandile has developed a sustainable and customer-focused business model that serves households, tuckshops, and businesses alike.
